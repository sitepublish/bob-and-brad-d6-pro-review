Frequently Asked Questions

Can you use a massage gun before a workout?
Using a massage gun before a workout can be beneficial for muscle activation and preparation. A brief 30-second application per muscle group at a moderate speed setting can increase blood flow, improve range of motion, and prime the neuromuscular system for exercise. This pre-workout percussion therapy should complement, not replace, a proper dynamic warm-up routine.
Can children use a massage gun?
Massage guns are generally not recommended for children under 12 years old, as their developing musculoskeletal systems are more sensitive to percussive force. Older teenagers who are active in sports may benefit from gentle use under adult supervision, using the lowest speed setting. Parents should consult a pediatrician or sports medicine physician before allowing a child to use any percussion therapy device.
Is the Bob and Brad D6 Pro good for athletes?
The Bob and Brad D6 Pro is well suited for athletes due to its powerful motor, adjustable speed settings, and variety of attachment heads. Athletes use it for pre-workout activation, post-workout recovery, and addressing specific areas of tightness or discomfort. Its portability also makes it convenient for use at the gym or during travel to competitions.
